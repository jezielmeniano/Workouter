Workout Plan Overview

This 4-day workout plan is designed for weight loss (target: 45kg) and body toning, focusing on defined arms, small waist, abs, and lean legs. Each session lasts approximately 1 hour.

Duration: Long-term (6 months to 1 year)

Primary Goal

Weight loss to 45kg

Duration

1 hour sessions

Frequency

4 days per week

Equipment

Dumbbells & Kettlebell

10 min warm-up | 35 min strength | 10 min core | 5 min stretch

1 min
Bodyweight squats

2 min

Main Workout – 3 Rounds (35 min)

Goblet Squats
12 reps → Use 8kg kettlebell or 6kg dumbbell

12 reps → Use 6kg dumbbells

20 reps → Use 8kg kettlebell

10 reps/leg → Use 4kg dumbbells for added resistance
