4-Day Home Workout Plan

A comprehensive kettlebell-focused routine for building strength and burning fat

1

UPPER BODY + CARDIO BURST

ARMS FOCUS
Warm-up (5-10 min)
  • W
    Arm swings, jumping jacks, shoulder rolls, arm pulses
Main Workout
  • 1
    Bicep curls (4kg or 6kg dumbbells)
    3×12-15
  • 2
    Overhead shoulder press (dumbbells)
    3×12-15
  • 3
    Tricep kickbacks (4kg dumbbells)
    3×12-15
  • 4
    Bent-over rows (kettlebell or dumbbells)
    3×12-15
  • 5
    Lateral raises (4kg dumbbells or lower)
    3×12-15
  • 6
    Kettlebell upright rows
    3×12-15
Cardio (15 min HIIT – 2-3 rounds)
  • C
    30s jump rope → 30s kettlebell swings → 30s mountain climbers → 30s squat to overhead reach
2

LOWER BODY + CORE

KETTLEBELL
Warm-up (5-10 min)
  • W
    Glute bridges, leg swings, squats, light lunges
Main Workout
  • 1
    Goblet squats (kettlebell)
    3×12-15
  • 2
    Kettlebell Romanian deadlifts
    3×12-15
  • 3
    Reverse lunges (dumbbells/bodyweight)
    3×12-15
  • 4
    Kettlebell sumo squats
    3×12-15
  • 5
    Calf raises (hold kettlebell or dumbbells)
    3×15-20
  • 6
    Glute bridges with weight (optional)
    3×15
Core Finisher (3 rounds)
  • C
    Plank 30s → 20 Russian twists (with KB) → 15 leg raises → 20 flutter kicks → 30s side plank (each side)
3

FULL BODY CIRCUIT + KETTLEBELL HIIT

INTENSITY
Warm-up (5 min)
  • W
    Dynamic stretches and light movement
Circuit (3 rounds – 40s on / 20s off)
  • 1
    Kettlebell deadlift to squat
  • 2
    Jump squats (or bodyweight)
  • 3
    Bent-over rows (dumbbells)
  • 4
    Burpees
  • 5
    Kettlebell swings
  • 6
    Push-ups (incline or full)
  • 7
    High knees
  • 8
    Dumbbell side lunges
  • 9
    Seated Russian twists (KB or DB)
Cool Down
  • C
    Stretch arms, hips, hamstrings
4

ARMS + ABS

TONING & SHAPE
Warm-up (5 min)
  • W
    Dynamic stretches and light movement
Arms (3 sets x 15-20 reps)
  • 1
    Hammer curls (4kg dumbbells)
    3×15-20
  • 2
    Overhead tricep extensions (use kettlebell)
    3×15-20
  • 3
    Front raises (dumbbells)
    3×15-20
  • 4
    Lateral raises (dumbbells)
    3×15-20
  • 5
    Arm pulses + circles (light dumbbell or no weight)
    3×15-20
Core Burnout (2 rounds)
  • C1
    30s Plank → 20 Bicycle crunches → 15 Toe touches → 20 Seated in & outs → 20 Oblique crunches (each side)
Cardio Core (2 rounds, 30s each)
  • C2
    Jumping jacks → KB swings → mountain climbers → high knees → skaters

Always consult with a fitness professional before starting any new exercise program. Remember to listen to your body and modify exercises as needed.
